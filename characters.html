Love

Love is a loveable character that always wants the best for people. She cares a lot for others around her and puts them before herself. Her hair stands out in a crowd and her favourite colour is pink!

Embarrassed

Embarrassed is an anxious mess and gets very embarrassed about being embarrassed. Despite his flaws his friends love him unconditionally. He faces challenges which tests his character.

Jealousy

Despite a mean exterior and sarcastic mannerisms, Jealousy does have a soft spot deep down. His friends understand him, and they accept him as he is. Sometimes he can be really nice and friendly!

Boredom

A sluggish character, Boredom speaks and moves very slowly. His favourite colour is blue. You can normally find him asleep on the sofa, or anywhere for that matter, with his favourite night cap on!

Excited

A very excitable, over-the-top character perfectly describes Excited. Although sometimes his personality can lead him into tricky situations from not thinking his friends always help him out!
